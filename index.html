Collabs Certify

Step 1: Upload Template & Draw Boxes

1. Upload your certificate image. 2. Click "+ Add Box" up to 3 times to create n1, n2, and n3 spaces. 3. Drag and resize them over the blank lines.

Step 2: Upload Student Data

Please upload a CSV file with columns matching your boxes (n1, n2, n3).

We have securely saved the exact positions of your signature boxes. Once your CSV is uploaded, click generate to start the batch processing.
